Project 2 — Solution

Automated compliance evaluation at scale

Step 01
Ingest & Resolve
Airflow DAG fetches completed calls every 30 minutes. For each call, resolves the company and which tests to run — configurable per client via Airflow Variables.
Step 02
Evaluate with LLM + Programmatic Checks
Transcripts are sent to GPT with versioned prompts from Langfuse. Hybrid checks compare LLM-extracted values (amounts, IDs) against ground truth in the database.
Step 03
Alert & Store Results
All results land in Redshift with full reasoning. Calls flagged as under_revision trigger Slack alerts with a direct link to the call for human review.
