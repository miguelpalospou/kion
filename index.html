Project #1

Legal Procurement Document Processing — 2023

🎯

Problem Statement

A small team of state lawyers must legally validate a massive volume of complex, high-risk procurement files. A single mistake can become an appeal, an audit finding, or a contract annulment. Critical data is trapped in unstructured documents with no standardized extraction pipeline.
⚙️

Solution

Desktop decision-support tool that automatically extracts, structures, and validates key contract elements:
  • VEC (Valor Estimado del Contrato)
  • Contract type, object & CPV classification
  • Award criteria & internal coherence checks
  • Traceable audit trail — final decision stays with lawyers
📐

Agent Architecture

  • Multi-format ingestion (PDF, DOCX)
  • NLP extraction + rule-based validation
  • Structured data warehouse with versioning
  • API layer for legal decision support
  • Data quality dashboards & alerts

Project #2

Call Center Audio Analytics Platform — 2025–26

🎯

Problem Statement

Companies rely heavily on phone calls to manage debt collection, payment follow-ups, and customer negotiations. Critical business intelligence is trapped in sensitive audio — because the data is personal, unstructured, and stored across multiple providers, companies focus on compliance rather than insight.
Step 01
Ingest & Classify
Multi-provider audio ingestion pipeline. Raw audio is cleaned, normalized, and clustered using acoustic features (MFCC) and metadata tagging for automatic categorization.
Step 02
Transcribe & Segment
Speaker diarization with timestamp segmentation. Structured conversation data stored in data lake with full-text indexing for downstream analytics.
Step 03
Review & Extract Insights
NLP analysis for intent detection, sensitive content flagging, risk scoring, and quality metrics. Results fed to real-time dashboards and automated alerting systems.
